Who we are

In 1890, Sri Manchukonda Kodanda Ramaiah started jewellery business in old town area of Visakhapatnam. The business was succeeded by Sri M.Appalaraju and later Sri M.V. Singaraiah. This business was taken over by Mr. M.Y. Subramanyam (Raja) in 1982, after completion of his education.

M Y Subramanyam lovingly called Raja, along with his brother M A Sri Ranga moved this business from old town to Rama Talkies Road and started it with the name and style of "MVS Varna".

Having been in a business family and that too in a family with Bullion Trading as business, Mr. Raja got the exposure from a very early age. Through his interaction with his grandfather and his father, he acquired all the knowledge and technique required to be in the Bullion Business.

Today MVS Varna is one of the most trusted gold Jewellery show rooms in Visakhapatnam.

With over 30 years of experience and his strong beliefs of meticulous market analysis, reliability of service and quality customer care as the procure in bullion trade; Mr. Raja partnered VBC in the year 1998.

He is a member of the Indian Bullion and Market Association (IBMA) and deals in both the MCX and COMEX Markets.
